Exhibitions

COSMOLOGY

Explore the wonders of space!

Learn about stars, planets, and constellations in our exhibition The Sky Above Us. Discover how people used the stars to travel and how we explore our solar system today.

EVOLUTION

Find out where life began and how animals and people changed over time.

Know more about evolution and meet amazing creatures from the past, like woolly mammoths, dodos, and dinosaurs.

BIOLOGY AND MEDICINE

Explore the tiny world of cells, germs, and the human body.

See how medicine has changed lives, from penicillin to heart transplants, and how animals have helped scientists make new discoveries.

ROBOTICS AND AI

Encounter the world of robots and artificial intelligence.

Get to know how AI helps at home and how robots work in industry. You can even meet Rob the Robot and shake his hand!

ECOLOGY

Understand how people affect our planet and how we can build a better future.

Learn about global warming, pollution, and endangered animals, and discover simple ways we can help protect Earth
